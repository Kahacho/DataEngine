Community Health Units

  • Even though majority of community health units in every county are functional, there still exists a significant number of health units that are either non-functional, semi-functional, or closed.
  • This contradicts with what was envisioned in Vision 2030 since the objective was to provide a functional, efficient, and sustainable health infrastructure network in the country.
  • For instance, counties such as Machakos, Makueni, Isiolo, and Kitui have more than half of their community health units semi-functional which is worrying.
