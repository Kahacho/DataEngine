Places of Birth

  • Over 50% of births in the Northern counties such as Marsabit, Wajir, Turkana, and Mandera were in non health facilities. Therefore, there is still limited access to maternal healthcare in the Nothern region of the country.
  • Despite the Linda Mama program being in place to ensure free maternity services in public hospitals, there exists quite a significant number of births that occurred in a non-healthy facility with Narok and Nakuru recording the highest number.
