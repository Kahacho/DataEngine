Operation status of hospitals

  • Access to health services at the time of need is critical for any population. However, this is not the case as it can be seen in the map.
  • More than 70% of the counties have more than 80% of their hospitals not operating 24 hours daily, an implication of limited hospital access.
  • Counties such as Lamu, Isiolo, and Tana River appear to have the least number of hospitals operating whole day, late night, on weekends, and public holidays which should be of great concern.
