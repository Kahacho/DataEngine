Percentage change in Health staff

  • Only 20 counties including Kajiado, Nyeri, and Kisumu recorded an increase of core health workforce per 10,000 population between 2013 and 2019. On the other hand, counties like Embu, Nyandarua, and Garissa recorded a significant decline over the 6 years.
  • A possible explanation for this is that the rate of workforce increase has not been directly proportional to the population increase in the counties since devolution of health services.
